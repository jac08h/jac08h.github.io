When a scientist says "space is infinite and full of stars", she does not literally mean that the crystal sphere surrounding the Earth doesn't exist.
She is metaphorically referring to the infinitude of the human spirit, the limitless possibilities it offers, and the brightness and enlightenment waiting to be discovered.
