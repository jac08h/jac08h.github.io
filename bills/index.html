Bill Splitter

Calculate how to split bill(s) between people to minimize the number of transactions.

Person
Amount paid
Target percentage

The calculation works by iteratively pairing the person who needs to pay the most with the person who needs to be paid the most, and making a transfer between them. The transfer amount is the minimum of these two amounts. By this step, at least one balance is reduced to zero, and the process is repeated until all balances are zero.
